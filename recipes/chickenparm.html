Chicken Parm

Description

Chicken Parm with pasta of your choice

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Eggs
  • Penne or Spaghetti

    Directions

  1. Pound Chicken
  2. Dip chicken in eggs
  3. Put bread crumbs on chicken
  4. Fry chicken breasts
  5. Bake chicken with sauce and cheese
